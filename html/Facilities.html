Library

The college has a central library with a stock of approximately 50000 Books including some rare publications. Besides reference books, there are a good number of Journals and Periodicals in different disciplines of Arts, Commerce, Science, General Knowledge etc.

Hostel

The college has a girls' hostel attached to the college campus. It is a well furnished hostel with all basic facilities. The boarding capacity of the hostel is 124 and total number of vacant seats will be available after giving accomodation to old bonafied students. The seats are alloted to the students on the basis of their merit as well as the distance from their residence. The main purpose of the hostel is to cate the needs of girls' who hail from distant and also remote areas of the tinsukia district and neighbouring states of Arunachal Pradesh.

Canteen Facilities

The college has a well ventilated and fully furnished veg and non-veg canteen for providing hygienic eatable and delicious for students and teachers at an affordable rate.
